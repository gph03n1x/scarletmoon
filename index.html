Welcome to Scarlet Moon’s documentation!¶

This application creates an inverted index in the form of a binary tree for each letter (a, b, c, d, ...), digit (0-9).

Search operators: [:and:, :or:, :not:]

Searching for hurricanes :

```bash # hurricanes [*] Article :WEST TEXAS COTTON CROP UNSCATHED BY STORM

Document:reutersreut2-018.sgm tf-idf : 0.04846927182717779
[*] Article :UK SUGAR FACTORY CLOSES DUE TO SHORTAGE OF BEET
Document:reutersreut2-021.sgm tf-idf : 0.04326451780546743
[*] Article :CUBA SUGAR CROP SEEN AT LEAST SAME AS LAST YEAR
Document:reutersreut2-004.sgm tf-idf : 0.043119820421502655

```
